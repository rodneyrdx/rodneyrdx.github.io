Contract Resolution Process
Automated on every publish — zero manual negotiation
01
Identify Assets
All assets with revenue sharing declared are identified. External marketplace assets are excluded.
02
Sum Minimums
Total minimum participation is calculated across all included assets to check feasibility.
03
Verify Feasibility
If minimums exceed the pool, creator must expand the pool, remove, or swap assets.
04
Distribute Budget
Remaining budget is split proportionally by each asset's expansion capacity (max − min).

Creator Control

Unused Returns
Any unclaimed portion of the 30% asset pool automatically flows back to the game creator. Creators using only their own assets can earn up to 80% of total revenue.
